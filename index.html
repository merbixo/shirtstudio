Checkout & Wise

We use Printify for product printing and fulfillment. For payouts and receiving store income you can connect Wise (formerly TransferWise) to your account for low-fee transfers.

  1. Create a Printify account and connect a store (Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, or use Printify's mock storefront link).
  2. In Printify, set your payout method to Wise (if supported) or withdraw to your local bank via Wise details.
  3. On this static demo site, "Buy" buttons link to the Printify/merchant product page ‚Äî actual checkout will happen on the Printify/store platform.

Note: Wise is used for payouts; to accept Wise payments directly you'd need a backend (not included in this static GitHub Pages demo).
